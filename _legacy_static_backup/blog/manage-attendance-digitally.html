Attendance guide

How to manage attendance digitally

Digital attendance helps colleges reduce manual register work, improve accuracy and view absence patterns quickly.

Start by defining courses, batches, subjects and staff ownership. Faculty can mark attendance by class, while administrators review daily summaries, low-attendance students and department-level trends.

Benefits

  • Faster daily marking
  • Student-wise and batch-wise attendance history
  • Low attendance alerts and reports
  • Cleaner compliance records

CampusEdgePro includes attendance tracking as part of the full college ERP workflow.
